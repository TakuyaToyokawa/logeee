Introduction

Why is Reasoning Important?

Everyday, we are faced with situations where we have to make decisions, or make a point. Reasoning is the act of constructing thoughts into valid arguments or reasons for why one decsion should be made over another. As such, knowing how to reason properly is important to evaluate the situations we are in, and successfully respond to them to achieve the outcomes we want.

Deductive Reasoning

Facts Over Opinions

Deductive reasoning is when a person makes an arguement or decsion by using true facts.

Deductive reasoning is harder to disprove, as it uses indisputable facts, however, it is a more difficult reasoning to use in everyday life, as it requires you to know all the relevent details of the siutation to successfully argue your point.
